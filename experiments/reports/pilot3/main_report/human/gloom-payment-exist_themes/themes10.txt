Story 1
The current political climate left Rachel feeling a sense of gloom. She wondered what she had done to exist in a time where there was such divide in the world, a world filled with war, violence, and poverty. Rachel sought out a number of charities, where she learned that for a small payment she could contribute to efforts tackling the issues she cared about in the world. Rachel felt less gloomy, as she read the inspirational stories of the charities, and her outlook on the world was brighter.

Story 2
It wasn't all doom and gloom. Matt found many ways to exist without falling in despair. One small payment to a bartender in exchange for a pint of ale was one such way.
