Story 1
Princess Ella sat by her window, watching the gloom of the forest fade as the sun rose over the castle grounds. Her loyal horse, Starlight, waited patiently for her, eager for their morning ride. Ella had dreamed of riding beyond her kingdom, where legends spoke of a hidden realm that didn't yet exist on any map. The only payment required to enter was a heart full of courage and a will to succeed, two things Ella had by the bucketful.
