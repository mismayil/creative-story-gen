Story 1
The single mother rushed home as awaited the news of the latest lottery draw. Her ticket was kindly given to her by her son, Phil. Watching in excitement, she was on the edge of her seat as each number matched the draw thinking about all the payments she could make. In ecstasy, she leapt out of her seat as she discovered she won the jackpot! However, she soon learns that her son Phil was pranking her, plummeting her joy to an all-time low, all she felt was gloom as she wished her son ceased to exist.
