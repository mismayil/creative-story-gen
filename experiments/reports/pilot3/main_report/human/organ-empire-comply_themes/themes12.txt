Story 1
At the heart of the new empire, compliance was mandatory. You really had no choice if you wanted to live in the upper echelons of society. Every organ in your body was genetically modified to allow you to breathe the air, which became more polluted each year. Alongside these helpful modifications were some more sinister tweaks that allowed the administration to shut them down should you fail to fall in line and comply. The poor souls in the down world struggled to survive every day, but at least they knew freedom.

Story 2
There once was a very strange land called Augmentia. It was an empire that had been created by dictators and was an island based off of the coast of Argentina. Word travelled fast that the dictators - namely Putinito - had begun an organ trafficking trade. The people of Augmentia, unbeknownst to them, had been born on the island and brainwashed into the ways of the land. When a citizen of Augmentia reached the age of 20, they were enlisted into a type of military boot camp where they had to comply with strict instructions and a gruelling regime of work, exercise, and weapons training. Those who did not comply with instructions, or tried to rebel against the bootcamps leaders, were never seen again by their families. It has been an enduring mystery where the rebels ended up, and Putinito had a mysterious job
