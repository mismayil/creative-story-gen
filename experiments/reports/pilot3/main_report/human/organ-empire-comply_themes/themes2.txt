Story 1
After the emperor fell ill, he desperately needed an organ transplant. Soldiers were ordered to scour the vast lands of his empire to find a suitable donor. Once found, they had no choice but to comply.

Story 2

The student dutifully removed the organ from the body as requested and placed it in the organ transplant box. Story 4
My uncle decided to donate his organs when he died, which was a very noble thing to do. Sadly on January 7th he fell off his bike and passed away. However many of his organs were able to be donated and his lovely young apprentice who worked in his shop, the Empire of Sweets needed a lung transplant and was able to benefit from my uncles donation. We were a little worried that in order to comply for my uncles lung the shop assistant would not match certain criteria but fortunately he did and all was well.
