Story 1
Not a long time ago and not in a galaxy far far away. No, this story is a lot different than star wars but shares certain elements such as tyranny, oppression, and depreciation of society. The lust for energy, petrol in particular, led to the long-anticipated world war. People tried to stop using vehicles powered by high-carbon emitting substances like diesel, but that was not of use. After a certain point, even though many machinery switched to electricity-fueled engines, people could not find sufficient ways to pump the engines with electricity.
