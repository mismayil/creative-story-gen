As the giant defender came flying into the tackle, the entire stadium erupted in protest. 'That's a disgusting challenge, you have to send him off referee!' As the opposing players surrounded the referee, he produced only a yellow card from his pocket, much to the outrage of the opposing captain. While the captain protested, the referee calmly explained his decision: 'by the letter of the law, that tackle is only a booking' he said
