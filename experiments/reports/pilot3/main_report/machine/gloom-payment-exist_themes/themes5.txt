Story 1
In a world where emotions were currency, Zara found herself bankrupt of joy, her soul overdrawn on gloom. She trudged through the neon-lit streets of New Sentiment, searching for a way to make a payment on her mounting melancholy debt. As she passed a dimly lit alley, a shadowy figure whispered, "Psst, wanna buy some existence?" Intrigued, Zara approached, only to discover a cosmic vending machine that dispensed realities like snacks. With trembling fingers, she inserted her last coin of despair and selected "Alternative Universe #42," where sadness didn't exist and happiness was free for all.

Story 3
In the dimly lit corner of the abandoned arcade, where the only sound was the faint hum of forgotten machines, a peculiar vending machine stood. It was said that for those who could decipher its cryptic payment system, it would grant the existence of something they had only ever dreamed of. With a mix of trepidation and hope, Alex inserted a handful of antique coins, each one a relic from a bygone era, into the slot. As the machine whirred to life, casting eerie shadows across the peeling wallpaper, a sense of anticipation filled the air. And then, with a soft click, a small, glowing orb appeared in the dispensing tray, pulsating with the promise of a new reality yet to exist.

Story 4
In a world where existence was a luxury, the gloom of non-payment hung heavy. The Exist-o-Matic, a device that granted life, demanded a hefty payment. Those who couldn't afford it existed in a state of perpetual gloom, their lives a mere shadow of reality. One day, a brave soul discovered a glitch in the system, allowing them to exist without payment. The gloom lifted, and a new era of free existence began.
